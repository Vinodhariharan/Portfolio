Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

This is a personal portfolio website. It does not have user accounts, sign-up forms, or collect any personal information directly. This policy explains what data is collected automatically and by which third-party tools.

Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects anonymised data including pages visited, time spent, device type, browser, and approximate geographic region. This data is processed by Google and is not personally identifiable. You can opt out by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser.

Blog

The blog section is powered by Supabase (hosted PostgreSQL). Only published post content is read by visitors. No visitor data is written to the database.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to distinguish users and sessions. No other cookies are used by this site. Dark mode preference is stored in your browser's localStorage, which never leaves your device.

Third-party links

This site links to GitHub, LinkedIn, YouTube, and LeetCode profiles. These sites have their own privacy policies and are not covered by this document.
